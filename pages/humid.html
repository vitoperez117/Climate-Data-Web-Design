Latitude vs Humidity

Across latitudes this distributions is the same:

Majority of cities are in the range of 40-100% humidity.

The minority of cities have humidity in the range of 10-40%. Some cities within latitudes of 60 and 80° have 0% humidity while there are no cities within latitudes of -60 and -80° that have 0% humidity. However, it is possible that this is because there are more cities near the north pole and significantly less cities near the south pole.

Latitude is not a strong determinant of humidity.
