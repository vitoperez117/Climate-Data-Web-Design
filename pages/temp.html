Latitude vs Max Temperature

Apparent trend resembles a bell curve that peaks at a range of 90-95° F between latitudes -20° and 40°. Cities in latitudes 20° below and above the equator occupy an average temperature range of 60-80° F.

Cities in latitudes south of the equator between -20° and -60° occupy a temperature range of 40° F- 95° F. We see a drop in the minimum temperature further from the equator. Cities in latitudes north of the equator between 20° and 40° occupy a temperature range of 40° F- 90° F, with an outlier around 20° F Cities in latitudes north of the equator between 40° and 60° occupy a temperature range of 10° F- 70° F Between latitudes 60° and 80° north of the equator, the temperature range is the lowest, between -5° F and -60° F.
